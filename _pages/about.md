Hi! I'm Greg Xie, a roboticist at Google Deepmind. I was previously at the Robotics and AI Institute (fka The AI Institute, the Boston Dynamics AI Institute) where I worked on hand and wrist design for the [AthenaZero](https://rai-inst.com/resources/blog/bimanual-robot-for-dynamic-manipulation/) robot.

I completed my Masters at MIT with Daniela Rus. Before that, I completed undergraduate degrees in Mechanical Engineering and EECS at MIT.
